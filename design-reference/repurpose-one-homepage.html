What you get

Built for creators who actually ship.

Platform-native copy
Not a simple repost. The AI rewrites your hook, angle, and format to feel native on every platform — LinkedIn gets professional, Twitter gets punchy, Reddit gets real.
One-click publishing
Connect your accounts once. Hit "Post" directly from the dashboard. No switching tabs, no copy-paste, no login juggling. Your whole distribution in one place.
Tone control
Choose from 6 tones — Professional, Casual, Witty, Storytelling, Educational, Bold. Your voice, adapted to each platform's culture and audience expectations.
Smart scheduling
Queue your posts for peak engagement times. Platform-specific optimal windows, auto-detected from your audience data. Set it and forget it.

How it works

From one article to six platforms in 30 seconds.

01
